BlastOn Deathmatch

Duck, Dive, and Weave like Neo in this fiercely competitive bullet hell.

Tournament structure:

  • A match is a head-to-head battle against an opponent with a 2 minute timer. Timeouts awarded to player in the lead
  • Participants will compete in a set of best of 3 matches for each round in the tournament
  • Double elimination, means that you are eliminated from the tournament if you lose 2 sets

Time

  • To ensure you can enjoy the conference and tournament to the fullest, this tournament has been structured in waves.
  • A wave refers to a group of 8 players who will play several sets against each other as a mini bracket in rapid succession
  • The top players in the group will go up against the top players of other waves later in the day.
  • We have a schedule of these playoff matches so you can enjoy the conference without worrying about when we'll call you.
  • Players can join at the start of any wave, but slots fill up quickly so don't wait until the end day!

Propagation (Coop)

Team up with a friend in this survival horror shooter

Tournament structure:

  • This is a rolling high score tournament. Similar to a classic arcade, the winner is the team with the highest score at the end of the conference
  • Individuals and teams can enter multiple times, but must purchase additional entry tickets to do so (like adding coins into the arcade)
  • Solo play is permitted, but we recommend teaming up for the best experience
  • There is a hard cap of 15 minutes on play time. If you are eliminated before that, you may try again for free, but you will still be capped at 15 total minutes per tournament entry
  • Higher difficulties give more points

Time

We will have stations running Friday/Sunday every 15 minutes

Superhot VR

The perfect game for anyone who's ever wished for better reflexes, time only moves when you do.

Tournament structure:

  • This is a rolling high score tournament. Similar to a classic arcade, the winner is the team with the highest score at the end of the conference
  • Individuals can enter multiple times, but must purchase additional entry tickets to do so (like adding coins into the arcade)
  • The game is played on the 10 minute challenge
  • There is a hard cap of 15 minutes on play time. You are welcome to take the first 5 minutes to get your bearings before starting the challenge

Time

We will have stations running Friday/Sunday every 15 minutes
